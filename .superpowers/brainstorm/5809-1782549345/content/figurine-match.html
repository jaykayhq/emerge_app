What Changed From the Neon Version

Matching Your Reference

  • Thick solid limbs — capsule geometry, not thin lines
  • Matte finish — high roughness, low metalness, no emission
  • Spherical heads with no facial features
  • Soft ambient lighting — big area light, soft shadows
  • Monochromatic base (customizable per archetype)
  • Volume & weight — they look like real figurines

Implications

  • 3D is now the right path (2D canvas can't fake this volume)
  • Limbs are capsules, torso is a tapered capsule, head is a sphere
  • Skinning needed for smooth deformation at joints
  • Animation via skeletal bones + mesh deformation
