Stickman Avatar — Concept Overview

A fully procedural, customizable stickman to replace the current PNG-based avatar system

What You're Looking At

This stickman is rendered entirely via CustomPainter — no image assets, no bundles. Every part (head, spine, arms, legs) is drawn as code-driven tapered bone quads with neon glow, rim lighting, and gradient fills. Results in a premium "neon silhouette" aesthetic.

     ○     ← Head (filled circle + neon ring)
     |      ← Neck bone
    /|\    ← Chest + Arms (tapered quads)
     |      ← Spine
    / \    ← Legs
   /   \   ← Feet
