How should notifications speak to you?

A

Persona First (Archetype)

Messaging focused on your Archetype (e.g., "Athletes don't skip workouts"). Consistent and role-focused.

B

Skill First (Attribute)

Messaging focused on the Attribute (e.g., "Increase your Strength today"). Practical and progress-focused.

C

Hybrid Mastery

Messaging that blends both (e.g., "As an Athlete, your Strength is your foundation"). Deeply personalized but more complex.

Hybrid Benefits

  • Maximum personalization
  • Higher identity reinforcement
  • Feels bespoke to every habit

Hybrid Costs

  • Higher cognitive load for reminders
  • Requires more complex template logic
