Emerge World Map Restructure

Select a design approach for the new Timeline to World Map to Attribute Screen flow.

A

The Immersive Portal (Recommended)

Timeline to Map: Hero zoom directly to the World Map orb.

Orb Experience: True 3D Fragment Shader with cracks and glow. Rotate via pan gestures. Tap 7 times to reveal the hidden mini-game.

Attribute Flow: Tapping an attribute in the ring pulls it to the center, pushing the orb back, then expands into the Attribute Screen.

Visuals: Deep psychological connection. The cracks represent entropy being fought off by habit completion.

B

The Constellation Flow

Timeline to Map: Standard fade-in, but the habit's associated attribute immediately pulses and draws a line to the orb.

Orb Experience: 3D shader orb. Mini-game triggered by spinning the orb extremely fast rather than tapping.

Attribute Flow: Tapping an attribute icon draws a glowing constellation line from the orb to the icon, then fades into the Attribute Screen.

Visuals: Focuses on the interconnectedness of attributes.

C

The Direct Nexus

Timeline to Map: Slide transition from Timeline to World Map.

Orb Experience: Interactive 3D orb with swipe gestures. Mini-game on 7 taps.

Attribute Flow: Tapping an attribute icon performs an immediate slide transition to the Attribute Screen. Less animation overhead, highly responsive.

Visuals: Clean and minimal. 3D styled attribute nodes that clearly display name and color.
