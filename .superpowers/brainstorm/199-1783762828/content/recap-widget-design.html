Design Notes

  • Compact height: ~80px total — sits between TodayArcCard and "Today's Habits" header
  • Stats: Shows habits completed this week, current streak, and XP earned this week — all pulled from already-loaded user stats (no extra Firestore reads)
  • Avatar icon: Small gradient circle in top-right corner (user photo or fallback icon)
  • Tappable: Tapping anywhere navigates to /recap (full weekly recap screen)
  • Visual style: Subtle gradient background + accent border matching the app's existing glassmorphism design language
  • No live recap needed: Stats come from userStatsStreamProvider which is already watched by the timeline screen
