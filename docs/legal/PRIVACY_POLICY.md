# Privacy Policy — Emerge

> **Last updated:** [DATE]

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Data
- Email address and display name (via Firebase Auth)
- Authentication provider (Google, Apple, Email)

### 1.2 Usage Data
- Habit creation, completion, and streak data
- Gamification progress (XP, levels, world state)
- Onboarding selections (archetype, identity attributes)

### 1.3 Device & Analytics
- Device type, OS version, app version
- Firebase Analytics events (anonymized)
- Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics

### 1.4 Subscription Data
- Purchase status and entitlements (via RevenueCat)
- We do NOT store payment card details

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- Provide and improve the Emerge app experience
- Calculate gamification stats and streaks
- Generate AI-powered reflections and insights
- Send push notifications (opt-in only)
- Analyze aggregate usage patterns

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- All user data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore)
- Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
- API keys are stored server-side in Google Cloud Secret Manager
- We follow Firebase's security best practices

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal data. Data may be shared with:
- **Firebase/Google Cloud** — infrastructure provider
- **RevenueCat** — subscription management
- **Google AdMob** — for free-tier ad display (anonymized identifiers)

## 5. Your Rights

You may:
- Export your data (contact support)
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Opt out of analytics collection
- Manage notification preferences in Settings

## 6. Children's Privacy

Emerge is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes via in-app notification.
