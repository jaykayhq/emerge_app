Overlay concept: soft smoky nebula aura, 3 to 5 cel-shaded flat wispy smoke shapes rising from ground level and dissolving upward, each wisp is an organic irregular shape curling softly upward, pale blue-grey fills #B0BEC5 at 25 to 30 percent opacity, bold ink outline 1px only on each wisp, no glow no particles just cool grey smoke on full transparency.
